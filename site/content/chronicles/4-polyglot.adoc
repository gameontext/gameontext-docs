Our team has nothing against JavaScript, but Java is what we know. When attempting
something big and new, we felt it was a good idea to constrain the number of
moving parts in the air. In this case, we stuck with a language we were
familiar with for the first core services.

The Node.js room brought JavaScipt to the backend. More will follow.
